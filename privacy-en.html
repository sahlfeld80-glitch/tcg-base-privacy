Privacy Policy for the app “TCG Base”

Last updated: 21 November 2025

1. Controller

Sascha Ahlfeld
E-mail: schattenwolf-studio@gmx.de

This privacy policy applies to the mobile application “TCG Base”.

2. General information

We take the protection of personal data very seriously. Data is processed solely for the purpose of providing and improving the features of TCG Base. The app can be used offline and without an account, with limited functionality.

3. What data do we process?

3.1 Local data on your device

The following data is stored locally on your device, among others:

  • card collections and deck lists
  • filter, sorting and display settings
  • statistics (e.g. drafts, matches, Commander sessions)
  • caches for card data, sets and images

These data remain on your device unless you use online sync or backup features.

3.2 Supabase (account & cloud sync)

For login, accounts and optional cloud features (backups, sync, online lobbies) we use the service “Supabase”. The following data may be processed:

  • e-mail address
  • hashed password (no plain-text password)
  • user ID (UUID)

Depending on your usage, the following data may also be synchronised:

  • collection data and deck lists
  • backup and export data
  • Commander tracker online sessions
  • draft and tournament lobbies

Service provider: Supabase, Inc., 970 Toft Street, San Francisco, CA 94122, USA
Privacy policy: supabase.com/privacy

Transfers to the USA are based on appropriate safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses under Art. 46 GDPR).

3.3 RevenueCat (management of premium subscriptions)

We use “RevenueCat” to manage in-app purchases and premium subscriptions. The following data may be processed:

  • internal user ID
  • purchase and subscription status (e.g. active, expired)
  • transaction information provided by the respective app store (e.g. product ID)

Payment data (such as credit card information) is processed only by the respective app store and is not stored by us.

Service provider: RevenueCat, Inc., USA
Privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

3.4 Google AdMob (advertising / reward ads)

TCG Base uses “Google AdMob” to display ads, especially reward-based ads that may unlock temporary features in the app. The following information may be collected:

  • device information (operating system, app version, language)
  • advertising ID of the device
  • truncated IP address
  • interactions with ads (e.g. impressions, clicks, completed rewards)

Service provider: Google Ireland Limited, Gordon House, Barrow Street, Dublin 4, Ireland
Privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

For children and younger users, only ads from Google Play certified ad networks suitable for this age group are shown. No advertising profiles are created for children, and no personalised advertising is displayed to them.

3.5 Server-side verification (reward SSV)

When a reward ad has been watched completely, a server-side verification (SSV) may be triggered. The following data may be sent to our Supabase backend:

  • user ID
  • type of unlocked feature (e.g. specific feature/pass)
  • expiry time of the unlock

No additional personal data is processed in this context.

3.6 Device permissions

The app may use the following permissions, only with your consent:

  • Camera: to scan QR codes or cards (if you use these features).
  • File access: to import/export decks, CSV files or backups.

You can change these permissions at any time in your device’s system settings.

4. Children and young users

The app can be used by users of different age groups. Content and ads are designed to be suitable for younger users as well. We do not create advertising profiles for children and do not show them personalised ads.

We recommend that creating an online account and purchasing premium content should only be done by adults or with the consent of a parent or guardian.

5. Legal bases (GDPR)

  • Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR – performance of a contract (provision of app features)
  • Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR – consent (e.g. for certain analytics or advertising)
  • Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR – legitimate interests (e.g. stability, security, abuse prevention)

6. Storage period and deletion

Local data on your device can be deleted at any time via the app or by uninstalling the app. Cloud data (account, collections, backups) will be deleted when you close your account or request deletion by e-mail, unless statutory retention obligations require longer storage.

7. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have, among others, the following rights:

  • right of access to the personal data we process about you
  • right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • right to erasure (“right to be forgotten”)
  • right to restriction of processing
  • right to data portability
  • right to object to certain processing activities
  • right to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise your rights, you can contact us at: schattenwolf-studio@gmx.de

8. Scryfall and card data

TCG Base uses card data, set information, images and in some cases price information provided by the platform Scryfall.

TCG Base is an unofficial app and is not affiliated with or endorsed by Scryfall or Wizards of the Coast. All trademarks, logos and card names are the property of their respective owners.

No personal user data is sent to Scryfall when using card data within the app.

9. Changes to this privacy policy

We may update this privacy policy if the app’s features change or if legal requirements are updated. The latest version will always be available at this URL.
